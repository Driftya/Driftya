### Support

If something isn’t working, or you have a question about how Driftya works:

- Use **GitHub Issues** in this repository.

You don’t need to be technical.
Just describe what you were trying to do and what happened instead.

A few notes:
- There’s **no guarantee of fast replies**.
- Everything is read.
- Please avoid posting private note content, emails, or personal info.

Thank you for helping make Driftya calmer and safer.
